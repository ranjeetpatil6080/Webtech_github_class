Hinjewadi IT Park

  • Hinjawadi (also incorrectly spelt Hinjewadi) is a suburb in Pimpri Chinchwad on the outskirts of Pune, India. It houses the Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park, a large tech and business park spread over 2,800 acres, which was built by the Maharashtra Industrial Development Corporation. The IT Park is home to over 800 IT companies of all sizes
